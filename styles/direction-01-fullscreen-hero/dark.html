The Cost of Waiting

Every hour you wait, the damage spreads.

Water travels deeper into framing. Smoke residue bonds to surfaces. Mold begins colonizing within 24 to 48 hours. The right move is the fast move.

Hour 1 to 12

Active spread. Water tracks along studs and joists. Smoke residue settles into porous materials. This is the time-sensitive containment window.

Hour 12 to 48

Microbial growth begins. Soft materials absorb permanently. The insurance documentation window starts to close. Action shifts from extraction to remediation.

Day 3 and beyond

Structural compromise. Mold colonies established. Restoration cost multiplies. Reconstruction scope expands across walls, ceilings, flooring, and content.
